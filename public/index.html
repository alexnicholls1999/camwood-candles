    About Us

    "A shining light in the dark"

    Hello my name is Carol, I make candles and the founder of Camwood Candles. I discovered my passion for making candles after attending a candle making course in the Ashdown Forest in 2017. After researching different types of wax I fell in love with Beeswax. I founded Camwood Candles in 2018 to create beautiful candles, promoting the use of Beeswax which is a natural, pure, nontoxic wax with a clean, bright burn.

    Natural Substance.

    Beeswax is a completely all-natural substance straight from Mother Nature herself. It is the purest and most natural of all waxes with no additives.

    Burn Efficency.

    Beeswax burns longer, cleaner and brighter than any other candle.

    Native.

    Beeswax is the only wax that is essentially used in its native state, with only filtering for processing.

    Better air quality.

    Natural beeswax candles can provide an important ingredient for a healthier home. They eliminate bacteria, dust, pollen, pet dander and pollutants from the air.
