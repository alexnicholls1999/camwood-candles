Natural Substance.

Beeswax is a completely all-natural substance straight from Mother Nature herself. It is the purest and most natural of all waxes with no additives.

Burn Efficency.

Beeswax burns longer, cleaner and brighter than any other candle.

Native.

Beeswax is the only wax that is essentially used in its native state, with only filtering for processing.

Better air quality.

Natural beeswax candles can provide an important ingredient for a healthier home. They eliminate bacteria, dust, pollen, pet dander and pollutants from the air.
