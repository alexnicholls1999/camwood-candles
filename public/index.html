About Us

"A shining light in the dark"

Hello, my name is Carol and I make candles. In 2017 I attended a candle making course and fell in love with beeswax. Beeswax is a natural wax produced by the honey bee and I loved the idea that nature was helping me make my candles, so in 2018 I founded Camwood Candles. I have created some beautiful beeswax candles for everyone to enjoy and last year expanded my beeswax range to include lip and hand balm and soaps. I hope you enjoy browsing through my beeswax range.

Natural Substance.

Beeswax is a completely all-natural substance straight from Mother Nature herself. It is the purest and most natural of all waxes with no additives.

Burn Efficency.

Beeswax burns longer, cleaner and brighter than any other waxes.

Native.

Beeswax is the only wax that is essentially used in its native state, with only filtering for processing.

Better air quality.

Natural beeswax candles can provide an important ingredient for a healthier home. They help eliminate bacteria, dust, pollen, pet dander and bacteria from the air when burned.
